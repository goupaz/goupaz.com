<div style="padding: 50px;">
     Overall padding
</div>

<div style="padding-left: 50px; padding-right: 50px;">
     Individual side padding
</div>

<div style="padding: 50px 25px 50px 25px;">
     Four side padding in same line
</div>

</div>

</div>

</div>
